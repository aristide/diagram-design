Medallion architecture · Bronze → Silver → Gold

Three storage tiers that progressively refine raw data into trusted business assets. Each layer raises the quality bar — raw landing, cleansed tables, and curated aggregates ready for BI, ML, and APIs.

Bronze · Raw

Raw zone

  • Unmodified source data, landed as-is from every upstream system
  • Immutable — full replay history is always available
  • All schemas and formats accepted; no quality gates enforced
  • Cheap, high-volume storage (object store, Parquet or raw files)

Silver · Clean

Cleansed zone

  • Validated, deduplicated, and joined across sources
  • Schema enforced, null rates and freshness tracked
  • Query-able but not yet aggregated to business grain
  • Rebuilt from Bronze on schema change or quality failure

Gold · Serve

Curated zone

  • Business-level aggregates — KPIs, dimensions, fact tables
  • SLA-governed, tested, and documented for data consumers
  • Directly trusted by BI dashboards, ML feature pipelines, and APIs
  • Optimised layout (partitioned, Z-ordered) for fast analytical reads
