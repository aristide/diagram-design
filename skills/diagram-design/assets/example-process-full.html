Labour Force Survey · End-to-end workflow process

Eleven steps across six divisions — from sampling design through field collection, data quality, statistical analysis, and public dissemination. Each step shows its primary input, output, and tool.

Field Operations · Step 3

Data collection (focal)

  • 10,464 dwellings surveyed each quarter across Jamaica
  • Interviewers use Survey Solutions on tablets for direct data entry
  • Supervisors monitor progress and resolve field issues in real time
  • Survey Solutions used across all 3 stages: assignment, collection, HQ review

Data Quality · Steps 4–7

Three-pass cleaning pipeline

  • Pass 1 — HQ (Survey Services): consistency and completeness checks, approve or reject
  • Pass 2 — IT Division: SAS-based error detection and initial data cleaning
  • Pass 3 — Household Unit: secondary cleaning and analytical preparation
  • RD&E applies survey weights between cleaning passes 2 and 3

Dissemination · Steps 8–11

Analysis to publication

  • Household Unit produces statistical tables, Excel outputs, and press brief materials
  • Communications & Marketing reviews all outputs and approves infographics
  • Official results released via press conference and public statement
  • Final datasets and reports published to STATIN website and SDMX system
